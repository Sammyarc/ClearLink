How many participants can join ClearLink video conference?

ClearLink offers flexible meeting options. Depending on your subscription plan, you can hold meetings with varying number of participants. Our plans are designed to accomodate small team collaborations and large-scale webinars, ensuring you have the right fit for your needs.

Can I use ClearLink on multiple devices?

Absolutely, ClearLink is designed to provide flexibility and convenience. You can use ClearLink on multiple devices, such as computers, smartphones, and tablets, depending on the specific product or service you've subscribed to.

Is ClearLink compatible with other video conferencing platforms?

Yes, ClearLink is built with compatibility in mind. It can seamlessly integrate with various video conferencing platforms, making it a versatile choice for your communication needs. Whether you prefer Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, or other popular platforms, ClearLink can work alongside them to enhance your video conferencing experience.

How does CLearLink ensure the security of my video conferences?

At ClearLink, security is a top priority. We employ robust encryption protocols to safeguard your video conferences and data. Our systems are designed to protect against unauthorized access and eavesdropping. We regularly update our security measures to stay ahead of emerging threats, and we comply with industry standards and regulations.

Do I need to download any software to use ClearLink?

The answer depends on your specific use case. ClearLink offers both web-based and application-based solutions. If you prefer the convenience of using ClearLink directly through your web browser, you can do so without any downloads. However, if you opt for a dedicated application, you may need to download and install it on your device. ClearLink provides clear instructions and links to ensure a smooth setup process, regardless of your choice.

What kind of customer support does ClearLink provide?

ClearLink is committed to providing excellent customer support. We offer a range of support options, including email support, a knowledge base with helpful articles and tutorials, and phone support during business hours. Our dedicated support team is ready to assist you with any questions, technical issues, or inquiries you may have.
